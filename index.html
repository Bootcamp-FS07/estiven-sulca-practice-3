TIC TAC TOE

Start Game

Rules

Objective: Players aim to place of their marks (X or O) in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row on a grid.

Turns: Players take turns to place their mark in an empty square.

Winning: The first player to align marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.

Draw: If all squares are filled without a winner, the game ends in a draw.

Start: One player uses X and the other O.
